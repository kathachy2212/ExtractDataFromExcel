Drinko – Fresh Juice Bar Website - UI Design

Title: Drink – A Refreshingly Simple Juice Bar Website

Drink is a clean and vibrant juice bar website designed to attract health-conscious users, display the most popular juice items up front, and guide users smoothly toward making an order. The design emphasizes freshness, simplicity, and ease of use, with a layout that tells the brand story and builds trust through testimonials and clear contact options.

Tools & Technologies Used
  • Figma
Goals & Objectives
  • Display top popular juices right in the hero section to grab attention.
  • Allow users to place orders easily using a clear Order Now CTA.
  • Share the story of Drink to connect with visitors emotionally.
  • Show a menu section with details of available juices.
  • Provide a clean footer with essential links and subscription.
Features
  • Navbar (Header) & Hero Section
    • Sticky top navigation bar
    • Full-width banner image of fresh juice
    • Showcased: Top 3 most popular juices
    • Prominent "Order Now" CTA button.
  • About Us Section & Menu Section
    • A short brand story.
    • Grid layout with juice images, names, and ingredients.
  • Contact Us & Our Client
    • If user have some question then they able to ask via "Contact Us" section.
    • Our top clients with logo (If available) are shown
  • Footer with newsletter
    • Logo & tagline.
    • Quick Links
    • Newsletter
    • Social media links
User Flow / Journey Map
  • A[Visit Homepage] --> B[See Navbar + Hero Section]
  • B --> C[Explore Popular Juices]
  • C --> D[Click Order Now or Scroll Down]
  • D --> E[See About Us > Menu > Contact > Clients]
  • E --> F{Interested to Order?}
  • F -- Yes --> G[Click Order Now]
  • F -- No --> H[Subscribe or Leave Feedback]
Future upgrades could include
  • Add online payment & delivery tracking
  • Let users rate juices or mark favorites
  • Mobile app integration for faster access
