StockCore – Inventory Dashboard - UI Design

Title: StockCore – An Intelligent Sales & Inventory Analytics Dashboard

StockCore is a comprehensive inventory and sales performance dashboard designed for business owners and managers to quickly view and analyze critical business metrics. The goal was to transform raw stock and sales data into actionable insights using a clean, modern UI with real-time visualizations.
This dashboard helps users make informed decisions on product performance, profitability, and stock levels through intuitive data representation using graphs, cards, and dynamic tables.

Problem Statement

Business owners often lack a unified view of their sales and inventory performance. They toggle between spreadsheets and reports to track sales, stock, and profitability. StockCore aims to solve this with a real-time dashboard that summarizes everything in one place.

Tools & Technologies Used
  • Figma
Goals & Objectives
  • Build a dashboard that displays all key business KPIs in one place.
  • Offer clear visibility into sales, costs, and inventory trends.
  • Make the UI user-friendly, responsive, and visually appealing.
  • Enable filtering and drill-down into monthly or category-based data.
Features
  • Overview Cards
    • Total Sales
    • Total Costs
    • Sold Products
    • Stock In Hand
  • Visual Analytics
    • Category-wise Product Performance Graph
    • Top Selling Products Graph
    • Revenue vs Cost Chart
  • Detailed Lists
    • Monthly Purchase Order List.
    • Monthly Sales Order List
User Flow / Journey Map
  • User logs in to the dashboard.
  • Lands on the main analytics page.
  • Scrolls to graphs and product performance charts.
  • Explores monthly purchase & sales order lists.
Future upgrades could include
  • Export charts and reports to PDF/Excel
