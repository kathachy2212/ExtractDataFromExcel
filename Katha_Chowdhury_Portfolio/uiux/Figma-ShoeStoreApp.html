SoleMate – Step Into Style - UI Design

Title: SoleMate – A Category-Wise Shoe Shopping App

SoleMate is a modern e-commerce mobile and web application built specifically for shoe lovers. The goal of this project was to create a seamless and stylish user experience where users can easily browse shoes by category and complete purchases after signing up or logging in.

The focus was on clarity, smooth navigation, and guiding users from product discovery to purchase with minimal friction.

Tools & Technologies Used
  • Figma
Goals & Objectives
  • Enable users to explore shoes effortlessly by categories.
  • Require authentication before completing a purchase.
  • Ensure easy navigation from product view to checkout.
  • Build user trust and enhance conversion through clean UX patterns.
Features
  • Homepage & Category Listing
    • Casual, Formal, Sports, Sneakers, and more.
    • Eye-catching banners for new arrivals or seasonal sales
  • Product Detail Page
    • High-quality images, size options, pricing, and reviews.
  • Authentication Flow
    • Simple login/signup modal.
  • Cart & Checkout
    • Add to cart, quantity selection.
    • Checkout process only accessible after login.
Problem Statement

Users often leave e-commerce apps due to poor categorization and confusing login flows. The challenge was to design an app where users can explore products freely but are gently prompted to log in when they initiate a purchase.

User Flow / Journey Map
  • User visits homepage
  • Browses shoes by category
  • Taps on a product to view details
  • Clicks "Buy Now" → prompted to login/signup
  • After authentication → proceeds to checkout
  • Completes purchase
Future upgrades could include
  • Wishlist functionality
  • Online Payment
