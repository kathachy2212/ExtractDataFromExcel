A UI Design For Women's Beauty Parlour

Title: A Delightful UX Design for Women’s Beauty Services

A modern and elegant website for a women-centric beauty parlour. The purpose of this design was to create an intuitive and engaging experience for users to explore the parlour, view services, check expert profiles, and book appointments online. Unregistered users can explore the site freely, but online booking requires login or signup. Manual booking via phone is also available.

Tools & Technologies Used
  • Figma
Goals & Objectives
  • Create a visually appealing and feminine digital presence for the parlour
  • Allow users to explore services, experts, and blogs
  • Display parlour availability and opening hours dynamically
  • Enable users to leave testimonials and read blog content.
Features
  • Navbar & Hero Section
    • Elegant header with menus: Home, About, Services, Experts, Blog, Contact & Login / Signup button
    • Beautiful banner with a model/photo shoot background
    • "Book Now" btton and also Display of parlour open/close status with time info like: "We are open now – 10:00 AM to 8:00 PM"
  • About Us
    • Story of Parlours journey
    • Mission, vision, and commitment to women’s beauty
    • Real photos of salon interior and customer service.
  • Our Services & Highlighted Services
    • User's can see our available services.
    • Popular and most-booked services.
  • Our Experts, Partners & Testimonials
    • Profiles of certified beauticians and makeup artists.
    • Show logos of partnered brands
    • Client reviews with name, picture, rating, and comment
  • Blog, Contact Us, Footer
    • Each blog card includes image, title, snippet, and read more button
    • Simple form with Name, Email, Phone, Message
    • Newsletter subscription (Email input + Subscribe)
    • Quick Links (Home, Services, Contact, Blog)
    • Social Media Links (Instagram, Facebook, Pinterest)
  • Login/Signup Flow
    • Users must log in to book online
    • Otherwise, they can only explore or call to book manually
    • Newsletter subscription (Email input + Subscribe)
    • Quick Links (Home, Services, Contact, Blog)
    • Social Media Links (Instagram, Facebook, Pinterest)
User Flow / Journey Map
  • A[Homepage] --> B[Explore Services & About]
  • B --> C{Wants to Book?}
  • C -- Yes --> D{Logged In?}
  • D -- No --> E[Prompt to Login/Signup]
  • E --> F[Redirect to Login Form]
  • D -- Yes --> G[Online Booking]
  • C -- No --> H[Read Blogs or Leave Feedback]
Future upgrades could include
  • Category Wise Services implement
  • Live chat with beauty advisors
  • Calendar slot selection during booking
